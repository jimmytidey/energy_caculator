Notes

Mars Bars

This calculation assumes that the human body is 25% efficient at turning Mars bars into kinetic energy - this seems to be a common assumption. However, it is a very rough approximation. This estimate does not take into account metabolic base rate - the energy the human body consumes ever at rest.

Petrol

This assumes that petrol engines that is 20% efficient.

iPads

The weight shown is the weight of lithium ion batteries required to supply the total energy, using batteries that have the same performance as the iPad.

The graphic shows the number of iPads this is equivalent to.

It should be noted that iPads have an incredibly high performance battery - it weighs 148g and and has an output of 42 watt hours. This gives it a higher energy density than the best lithium ion batteries on Wikipeia - about 1 MJ/kg.

Calculation also assumes 95% efficient electric motors - this is fairly typical performance.
